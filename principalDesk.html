Gandhi Vidyalaya and Junior College, Kondha which became holy by the foot-touch of Vandaniya Rashtrasanta Shri. Tukdoji Maharaj was founded by Late. Shri. Tukaramji Motghare in 1953 for the purpose of reaching the Gyan-Ganga of Education in Rural area of Bhandara District, he started his first school in Kondha. Initially the school started by about 25 students has reached the strength Of this school till 1800. All this is attributed to the school of dormant, Honest Headmasters, Teachers, Non Teaching employees. Present Institutional Head, Adv. Anand Jibhakate's all-round commitment und profound ideas. In my rural areas, students should be trained and educated in the knowledge-building and in the events of competition, they will not be able to fall short in the comparison of the urban areas, The school has a well equipped multi-storey building with all physical comforts, science-technology laboratory, computer lab, e-learning education, beautiful garden, well-experienced lecturers and teaching staff filled With innovative educational activities and ideas for the bright future Of the students in Gandhi Vidyalaya and Junior College Kondha.
